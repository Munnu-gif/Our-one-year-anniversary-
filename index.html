Mere Dil Ki Baat...

Meri pyari jana, Jaun Elia ne kaha tha, "Jo guzri mujh par, wo tum par bhi guzregi..." lekin main chahta hoon ki aap par sirf khushiyan guzrein. Aap meri zindagi ka wo hissa hain jise main kabhi khud se alag nahi karna chahta. Is ek saal mein aapne mujhe sikhaya ki mohabbat kya hoti hai. Faraz ke lafzon mein kahoon toh, "Zindagi ko humne sirf aapke aane se mehsoos kiya hai." Aapki respect, aapki saadgi aur aapka mere prati wo 'Aap' wala lehza, mujhe har din aapka aur zyada deewana bana deta hai. Aap meri ibaadat hain. Main dua karta hoon ki aane wale saikdon saal hum aise hi saath rahein. Aapki aankhon mein kabhi aansu na aayein, aur aapke chehre ki muskurahat kabhi kam na ho. Aap jaisa koi nahi hai, aur na hi kabhi hoga.
