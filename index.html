Ehsaas-e-Muhabbat üåπ

Meri pyari Guduu, Aap sirf meri pasand nahi, aap meri ruh ki zaroorat ban chuki hain. Hamara safar shayad utna asaan nahi raha, par har ek mushkil ne humein aur kareeb laya hai. Aapke saath bitaya gaya har lamha mere liye kisi khazane se kam nahi. Jab bhi main girne lagta hoon, aapki himmat aur aapka bharosa mujhe phir se khada kar deta hai. Trust, yani vishwas, hamari is kahani ki wo buniyad hai jispar maine apne pure jahan ki khushiyan tiki hui dekhi hain. Aapne mujh par tab bhi bharosa kiya jab duniya mujh par sawal utha rahi thi, aur wahi aapki sabse badi khoobi hai jo mujhe aapka hamesha ke liye mureed bana deti hai.

Zindagi ke struggle mein aksar log saath chhod dete hain, par aapne dikhaya ki sacha pyar wahi hai jo andheri raaton mein bhi haath tham kar ujale ki taraf le jaye. Aaj hum jis success aur khushi ki taraf badh rahe hain, usme meri mehnat kam aur aapki duaein zyada shamil hain. Aapka mujhse itna care karna, chhoti-chhoti baaton par mera dhyan rakhna, aur har baar meri galtiyon ko maaf karke mujhe ek behtar insan banana, ye sab meri zindagi ki sabse badi jeet hai. Mere liye aapki importance lafzon mein bayaan karna namumkin hai, bas itna samajh lijiye ki aap meri wo 'Sukoon' hain jise main har namaz mein mangta hoon.

Mohabbat sirf 'I Love You' kehne ka naam nahi hai, balki ye aapke saath us izzat ka rishta hai jahan main har pal aapki salamti ki dua karta hoon. Aapka mujh par itna comfortable hona meri zindagi ka sabse bada reward hai. Main wada karta hoon ki duniya ki kisi bhi mod par, chahe kitne bhi struggle kyun na aayein, main aapka haath kabhi nahi chhodunga. Hum milkar har success ko celebrate karenge aur har gam ko adha baat lenge. Aap meri jaan hain, mera maan hain, aur mere liye sabse uncha samman hain. Isi tarah mere saath rahiye, kyunki aapke bina main bilkul adhura hoon. Main aapse be-had mohabbat karta hoon.
